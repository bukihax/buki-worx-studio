Our Story

Who We Are

BUKI-WORX STUDIO was founded with a simple mission: to create a space where women feel powerful, supported, and free to be themselves. We believe that every woman deserves to experience the transformative power of strength training, whether that's through pole fitness, silks, heels, or glutes and abs work. Our studio is a judgment-free zone where body positivity and personal achievement are celebrated.

Our Mission & Values

We empower women to build confidence, strength, and lasting community through dynamic, inclusive fitness classes. We value authenticity over perfection, progress over competition, and celebrate every milestone our members achieve. Respect, safety, and sisterhood guide everything we do. We're committed to making pole fitness, silks, and strength training accessible to women at every fitness level.

What Makes BUKI-WORX Different

Expert, Certified Instructors

Our team brings years of experience and genuine passion for helping you succeed safely and powerfully.

All Bodies Welcome

We celebrate diverse bodies, fitness levels, and backgrounds. Every woman deserves to feel strong and beautiful here.

Customized Progression

Whether you're starting your fitness journey or advancing your skills, we meet you where you are with scalable classes and modifications.

Meet Our Instructors

Jasmine Martinez

Studio Owner & Pole Instructor

Jasmine founded BUKI-WORX after discovering pole fitness transformed her confidence and strength. With 8 years of pole experience and professional certifications in fitness and pole instruction, she leads classes with energy and authentic care.

Zara Williams

Silks & Aerial Yoga Instructor

Zara brings grace and strength to every silks class. With a background in dance and aerial arts, she creates magical experiences that make you feel like you're flying while building incredible core and upper body strength.

Maya Patel

Heels & Strength Coach

Maya's classes blend fierce heels choreography with functional strength training. She specializes in helping women feel confident and powerful in their bodies while building muscle and cardiovascular fitness.
