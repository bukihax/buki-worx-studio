Private Bookings & Parties

Celebrate with BUKI-WORX

What's Included

Customized Class Experience

Choose your class type: pole fitness, heels, silks, or strength training. Our instructors design the session around your group's goals and vibe.

Professional Instruction

Certified instructors guide your group with personalized modifications and encouragement so everyone feels confident and included.
