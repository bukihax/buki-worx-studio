What to Bring & Arrival Tips

Arrive Early

Come 10-15 minutes before class. This gives you time to meet the instructor, ask questions, and get comfortable in the space.

What to Bring

Water bottle, small towel, and confidence! We provide everything else including grips, grip aids, and a supportive environment.

Wear Comfortable Clothes

For pole and silks: fitted clothes work best so instructors can see your form. For heels: your favorite athleisure outfit or workout gear.

First-Timer Friendly

No experience? Absolutely perfect! Our instructors are trained to help beginners feel supported and confident from day one.
