Our Classes

Find the perfect class for your fitness journey. All levels welcome, modifications provided.

Intro to Pole

Beginner

Perfect for first-timers! Learn fundamental pole techniques, grip strength, and basic spins in a fun, supportive environment. You'll build confidence and start feeling what your body can do.

Frequency: 2-3x per week

Pole Fitness

Intermediate

Build on foundational skills with advanced tricks, pole dancing combinations, and intense conditioning. This class combines strength training with artistic expression for a full-body workout.

Frequency: 2-3x per week

Heels Class

Beginner

Move with confidence, grace, and attitude in heels! Learn choreography that builds leg strength and glute activation while having a blast. Perfect for developing posture and feeling absolutely fierce.

Frequency: 1-2x per week

Silks

Intermediate

Float through the air with our aerial silk classes. You'll build incredible upper body and core strength while experiencing the magic of aerial movement. Expect to feel graceful, strong, and absolutely empowered.

Frequency: 2x per week

Glutes & Abs

Beginner

High-energy strength training focused on sculpting and toning. Combine resistance exercises, bodyweight movements, and cardio bursts for a challenging, rewarding full-body session.

Frequency: 1-3x per week
